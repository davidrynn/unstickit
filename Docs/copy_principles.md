# Unstuck — Copy Principles

## Purpose

Unstuck copy should help users move without pressure.

The product is a calm activation tool, not a task manager, habit tracker, therapy app, or accountability system.

---

## Core Rules

Use language that:

- treats stuckness and delay as normal
- preserves user agency
- keeps attention on one small action
- avoids shame, urgency, and productivity pressure
- makes stopping or letting go feel allowed

---

## Preferred Language

- **Start**
- **I'm still stuck**
- **Come back tomorrow**
- **Recent steps**
- **Let go**
- **Save**
- **Saved**
- **Unsave**
- **Make it smaller**
- **No need to solve it right now.**
- **This will disappear in 7 days unless saved.**

---

## Avoid

- **Tasks**
- **To-do**
- **Complete**
- **Overdue**
- **Priority**
- **Archive**
- **Productivity**
- **Streak**
- "Don't give up"
- "You can do it tomorrow"
- "Keep your streak alive"
- "We'll remind you to finish this"
- "Try harder tomorrow"
- "You failed to start"
